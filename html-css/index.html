About

Hello world. I'm a Freelance Digital Creative Director with over 12 years of experience in advertising and start-ups. I am fortunate to have worked with some of the most innovative brands, agencies and people in the world. I created campaigns, platforms, products, services and future concepts for global brands like Nike, Converse, KLM, Diageo, Rabobank and Heinz.

Before setting up my freelance practice, I was Digital Creative Director at the innovative communications agency Anomaly and Founder, Partner and Creative Director of Boondoggle, Digital Agency of the Year 2011. I've won over 30 (international) awards and honors, including Cannes Lions, Eurobest, Webby, FWA, ADC, SpinAwards, Emerce and Contagious.

I started my career as one of the youngest entrepreneurs in the Netherlands in 2001 and secured a $500k investment with my first start-up in 2004, age 21. During that time I also worked as a Producer of EDM, with my tracks being licensed on tens of thousands compilation CD's around the world. Nowadays you can book me (and if needed some additional friends) for freelance gigs to help out your agency, brand or start-up.

Services include, but are not limited to (1) Digital Creative Direction, (2) Branded Products and Services, (3) Digital Brand Campaigns and (4) Branded Platforms. With a focus on connecting digital and brand thinking I integrate both (long term) product and services with (short term) campaigns. Besides my advertising activities, I'm a micropreneur and CCO of Micro by Design, running small experiments and art projects which sometimes turn into start-ups.
