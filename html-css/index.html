About

Hello world. I’m Gaston Serpenti. Freelance Digital Creative Director and Micropreneur with over 12 years of experience in advertising and start-ups. I’ve had the privilege to have worked with and for some of the most innovative brands, agencies and people in the world. I’ve created campaigns, services, products and future concepts for global brands like Nike, Converse, KLM, Diageo and Heinz. I've been rewarded with over 30 (international) awards and honors, including Cannes Lions, Eurobest, Webby, FWA, ADC, Contagious, SpinAwards and Emerce.

Before setting up my freelance practice, I was Digital Creative Director at Anomaly and Founder, Partner and Creative Director of Boondoggle, Digital Agency of the Year 2011. I started my career as one of the youngest entrepreneurs in the Netherlands and secured a $500k investment with my first start-up in 2004, age 21. During that time I also worked as a Producer of EDM, with my tracks being licensed on albums accross the globe. Nowadays you can book me for freelance or interim gigs to create amazing digital work for your agency, brand or start-up.

I'm also Founder and ECD of Micro by Design. A network of micro product and service companies.

Services

With Serpenti & Friends I mostly work alone, but if needed, I can bring in some friends from a close collective of amazing talent I’ve met over the years. Services include, but are not limited to (1) Freelance Creative Direction, (2) Digital Brand Campaigns, (3) Branded Products and Services and (4) Branded Platforms. Always focussed on connecting digital work with the heart of the brand, creating an ecosystem of storytelling and searching for tweet value. Where possible I like to inject some product and service thinking into campaigns to create work that lasts just a little bit longer and delivers real value in people's lives.
